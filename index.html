LGBTQ+ Mental Health Awareness

Why is LGBTQ+ Mental Health Awareness important?

LGBTQ+ youth have been shown to face higher percentages of mental health issues.

The LGBTQ+ youth community represents a very large and diverse group of individuals. Many of these individuals have difficultly with low self-esteem, depression, anxiety, and many more. Mental health issues in LGBTQ+ youth is often overlooked and not thought about as it should be. Many schools and organizations do not have any focus in helping the LGBTQ+ youth community.

This is why we want to spread awareness for the LGBTQ+ youth community. Everybody deserves the mental health help that they need.

All facts were found at The Trevor Project or Mental Health America.
